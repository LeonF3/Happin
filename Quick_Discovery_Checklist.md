## üéØ Discovery Call Script

### Opening (2 min)
"Thanks for the opportunity. I've reviewed the proposal documents. To create an accurate estimate, I need to understand a few things about the current app and requirements."

### Current State (10 min)
"First, what's the current tech stack? And can I get access to review the codebase?"

### Features (15 min)
"What are the absolute must-have features for the first version? What can wait for v2?"

### Timeline & Budget (10 min)
"What's your target launch date? And what budget range are we working with?"

### Technical (10 min)
"Which platforms - iOS, Android, or both? Do you have App Store developer accounts?"

### Wrap-up (5 min)
"Based on this, I'll create a detailed proposal with timeline and pricing. Any questions for me?"
